Textbooks made easy


Campus Backpack was developed out of frustration with how students currently must look for their textbooks each semester. If they want to buy them online, they must search for the title, author, edition, and a bunch of other extraneous information to make sure they're getting the right one. Another option is to head to the campus bookstore and buy a used paperback that costs half their tuition. Yeah, right.

With Campus Backpack, users only need one piece of information to find the right textbook: the course they need it for. Click on one of the courses listed to find textbooks being sold for that course by other students. It's that easy.

Campus Backpack uses the Duke API to gather a list of courses, thus it is currently designed for only Duke students. However, I am currently working on improving the UI/UX and migrating the site to the MEVN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, Vue.js, Node.js) with hopes of deploying it for production by 2019. In the meantime, enjoy a sneak preview of what could be Craigslist for colleges!
